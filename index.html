How to Play

Goal

Defend your network against incoming threats by placing cybersecurity towers. Each threat that reaches the left edge breaches your defenses.

Controls

Click tower cardSelect a tower to place
Click grid cellPlace selected tower
Right-clickCancel tower selection
Hover over coinsAuto-collect coins
1-9Quick-select tower card
SpacePause / Resume
SToggle shovel (remove tower)
EscCancel selection

Tips

  • Start with Bitcoin Miners to build your economy early.
  • Firewalls are your basic defense — place them in every row.
  • Mines (Tripwire, EMP) are invisible but take time to arm.
  • Use VPN Shield to hide towers from threats.
  • On water levels, place Proxy Nodes first, then towers on top.
  • Save your Firewall Resets — they only trigger once per row!
